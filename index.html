About Us

Whistles Healthcare Solutions is a staffing partner dedicated to dependable coverage, credentialed talent, and patient-first care. We help hospitals, clinics, and long-term care facilities close gaps quickly—without compromising quality.

What We Stand For

  • Verified & Compliant: License checks, background screening, TB/Immunizations, and required trainings.
  • Speed with Standards: Average per-diem fill in under 24 hours across our Puget Sound network.
  • Patient-First Professionals: RNs, LPNs, and CNAs matched to unit acuity and culture.
  • Always Available: 24/7 coordinator support for last-minute or ongoing coverage.

Our Services

Flexible, on-demand staffing and support built for real-world census changes—per diem, contract, and full-time.

Registered Nurses (RNs)

Experienced RNs for Med-Surg, ICU, ER, Telemetry, and specialty units—aligned to your workflows.

Licensed Practical Nurses (LPNs)

Reliable LPNs for clinics, SNFs, and LTC—med pass, wound care, charting, and patient education.

Certified Nursing Assistants (CNAs)

Compassionate CNAs for ADLs, vitals, transfers, and rounding—improving throughput and satisfaction.

Shift & Schedule Coverage

Per-diem, short-term, and long-term coverage to stabilize staffing and reduce burnout.

Credentialing & Compliance

24/7 Coordinator Support

Dedicated point of contact for urgent needs, onboarding, and ongoing assignment support.
